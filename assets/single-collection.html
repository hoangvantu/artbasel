Kristin Grey Apple

Kristin Grey Apple is a New York-based video artist. She investigates the vertigo involved in being a body in a larger world of bodies. She received her MFA from Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts in Philadelphia and her BA in Studio Art from Davidson College in Davidson, North Carolina. She has participated in numerous US and International solo and group exhibitions.
